Who & Problem
  • Audience: UK engineering students seeking internships/grad roles.
  • Pain: Listings are scattered; eligibility is unclear and deadlines are easy to miss.
What it does (outcomes > features)
  • 5-second eligibility with clear badges & filters.
  • Never miss deadlines via due-in countdowns & one-tap reminders.
  • Weekly digest to spot new/reopening roles.
MVP scope (Version 1)

Simple table UI; eligibility badges/toggle; deadlines with “days left”; accurate apply links; verified listings.

Out of scope (Phase 1)

Calendar sync, notes on a role, manual tracking (applied + stage), sign-in/sign-up, motivation/streaks, hide closed roles, share role link.

Goals & how we’ll measure
  • Eligibility decision time ≤ 7s — timed task on the clickable demo (median across ≥10 target students).
  • Find-an-open-role ≥ 80% within 60s — usability test completion rate.
  • Apply click-through ≥ 30% — event tracking from role view to “Apply”.
  • Data freshness ≥ 90% — roles updated within 7 days (weekly audit).
Press Release & Frequently Asked Questions (PR/FAQ)
  • What’s new vs job boards? We optimise eligibility + deadlines first, with consistent sub-discipline tags and verified apply links.
  • Why UK engineering first? This doesn’t exist in the UK; I’m UK-based so interviews/pilots are easy; single-country focus keeps data clean.
  • Biggest risk & mitigation? Finding users: word of mouth at uni, YouTube/TikTok clips. Competitors: few direct, focus on simple fast verified data and deadline reminders. Scam links: link verification, report button.
