PRODUCT BRIEF

SteadyPaste — Accessible Toothpaste Dispenser

One-press, mess-free toothpaste for people with limited grip strength — consistent dose, no squeezing.

One-press actuation Consistent dose Refillable cartridge Easy-clean nozzle
Who & Problem
  • Audience: Adults with limited grip strength or hand tremors (e.g., arthritis, Parkinson’s) who want to brush independently.
  • Pain: Squeezing a tube is hard and messy; the amount changes each time, and people can end up needing help for a basic daily task.
What it does (outcomes > features)
  • Gets the same amount every time with a single, easy press—no fine motor control needed.
  • Cuts the mess with an anti-drip, easy-clean nozzle and cap.
  • Stays put with a non-slip base or wall-mount bracket so the press is stable.
MVP scope (Version 1)

Air-pump module drives a plunger inside a refillable cartridge to dispense a set amount; large press surface; one-way valve; dose-limiter; non-slip base or wall mount; easy-clean cap/nozzle.

Out of scope (Phase 1)

Motorised dispensing or sensors; phone/app features; variable dosing; compatibility with every retail tube (V1 uses our refillable cartridge for consistency).

Goals & how we’ll measure
  • High consistency: at least 9 out of 10 presses give the target amount, within ±10% of a “pea-sized” ~0.25 g dose.
  • Light push to use: works with a press force of ≤15 N (about the weight of a 1.5 kg object).
  • Quick dispense: delivers the dose in ≤1.5 s.
  • Easy on first try: ≥80% of target users can use it correctly with no instructions.
Press Release & Frequently Asked Questions (PR/FAQ)
  • What’s new vs. a squeeze tube? A one-press mechanism moves a plunger to give a predictable dose without squeezing.
  • Biggest risks & mitigations? Inconsistent dose (dose-limiter + check valve, set stroke length); nozzle clogging (cap + anti-drip geometry tests); pump durability (reinforced mounts + life-cycle testing).
  • What’s next? Looks-like/works-a-bit prototype to measure dose consistency and required force, then 5-user home trials to check independence and cleanliness.
