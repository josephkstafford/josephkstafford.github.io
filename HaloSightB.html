PRODUCT BRIEF

HaloSight — Firefighter Visor Display

See your crew through smoke, talk hands-free, and trigger a one-press Mayday when seconds matter.

AR HUD cues Hands-free comms One-press Mayday UK market first
Who & Problem
  • Audience: UK fire & rescue services (public-sector, station-level deployment).
  • Pain: In emergencies—especially in smoke and heat—crews can’t reliably locate team members who may be in danger or trapped; radios and manual signals are too slow or hard to operate with gloves.
What it does (outcomes > features)
  • Maintain team awareness with simple heads-up cues (status/position signal).
  • Speak hands-free while tools stay in hand.
  • Trigger Mayday instantly with a glove-friendly, one-press action and clear confirmation.
MVP scope (Version 1)

Helmet-mounted visor module; always-available voice; HUD status cues; glove-friendly Mayday button; directional awareness sensor; on-site fitment compatible with common UK helmets.

Out of scope (Phase 1)

Thermal imaging/vision enhancement; building floor-plans/indoor maps; autonomous indoor positioning accuracy guarantees; cross-border certifications beyond UK.

Goals & how we’ll measure
  • Time-to-Mayday ≤ 2s — timed task with gloves in drill house (median across ≥10 firefighters).
  • Hands-free comm success ≥ 95% — successful send/receive attempts during simulated incident.
  • Team contact maintained ≥ 90% — share of drill time with working team-status signal.
  • Adoption in pilot ≥ 2 stations — pilot rollout with station-level champions and feedback loop.
Press Release & Frequently Asked Questions (PR/FAQ)
  • What’s new vs. thermal imaging products? HaloSight focuses on communication & crew status first (AR cues + hands-free + Mayday) rather than vision enhancement alone, at a price designed for public services.
  • Why launch in the UK first? Clear initial customer set (49 regional authorities), simpler certification path (UKCA and related regs) and lower initial certification costs before expanding.
  • How is it priced? Cost-plus with tiered discounts for bulk orders; optional annual maintenance contract for quick fixes and uptime assurance.
  • Biggest risks & mitigations? Ruggedisation & reliability (burn tower and wet-environment testing); user adoption (on-site training and pilots); certification constraints (phase roll-out with pre-certified radio modules).
